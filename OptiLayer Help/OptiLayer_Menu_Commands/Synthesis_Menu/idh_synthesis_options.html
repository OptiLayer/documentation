Analysis and Synthesis Options

` <modifycolortarget.html>`__ ` <idh_menu_synthesis.html>`__ ` <idh_synthesis_options_method.html>`__

The Options menu items are generally the same in the Analysis and Synthesis Menus, except for selecting an optimization method (the last option is available only in the / Synthesis Menu).  Most are related to changing the format and contents of the graphics display. The selection of the optimization method may be used to provide better convergence of the optimization procedure.

The following options are available:

  • Selecting Optimization Method and Needle Options

  • Adjusting Scales

  • Selecting Characteristics To Be Plotted

  • Selecting Design Bar Presentation

  • Selecting Material Abbreviations and States

  • Trapping Options

  • Editing Header and Legend
