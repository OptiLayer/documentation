Table Simplify

` <compositionfromtable.html>`__ ` <idh_edit_layer_material.html>`__ ` <idh_database.html>`__

The “Table Simplify” command enables the reduction of the number of spectral points, albeit with a slight decrease in accuracy. Specify the new number of points at the top of the dialog. Any discrepancies will be immediately displayed beneath the curves, allowing you to quickly assess the impact on accuracy. Once you’ve achieved a desired balance between accuracy and the table’s complexity, the result can be saved to the current Substrate/Layer Material editor by clicking the “OK” button.
