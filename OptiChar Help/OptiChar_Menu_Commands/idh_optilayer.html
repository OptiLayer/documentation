OptiChar features a comprehensive online Help system designed to assist users in finding specific information on various topics. To search for specific information, simply click on the OptiChar Help command found in the Help menu. This will allow you to access detailed documentation and guidance tailored to your needs.

The Key Assignment command will display a list of keyboard shortcuts that you can use to enhance your productivity while using OptiChar.
For assistance with the OptiChar Menu and Toolbar, simply click on the respective menu item or toolbar button below to access helpful information about their functionalities.

You can also access a specific Help topic for any dialog window by pressing the Help button or the F1 key. This will provide you with detailed information and guidance relevant to the current window or feature you’re using, ensuring you have the support you need right at your fingertips.
